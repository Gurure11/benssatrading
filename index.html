About Us

Solly's Skills Driving Academy is a premier driving school committed to providing high-quality driver education to students of all skill levels. Founded by Mr. S. Montoedi, our academy has grown to become one of the most trusted names in driver training in the region.

Our mission is to equip learners with not just the technical skills to pass their driving tests, but with the knowledge and confidence to become safe, responsible drivers for life. We believe that proper driver education is the foundation of road safety.

What sets us apart is our team of patient, professional instructors who are passionate about teaching. We understand that every student learns differently, so we tailor our approach to meet individual needs. Whether you're a complete beginner or just need to brush up on your skills, we have the perfect program for you.
